<u>How To Play</u></p>

<p>

Overview:</p>

<p>

Minesweeper is a type of puzzle game that involves logic and a bit of luck. The game begins with a grid of blank cells, some of which are hiding a mine. The objective of the game is to place a flag (right click) over the location of each hidden mine as quickly as possible. Left clicks are risky as they reveal bombs, so if the player clicks on a bomb it&#39;s game over. If the player does not hit a bomb, it will reveal either a cell with a number, or a large span of blank cells with numbers at the edges. Once the player places a flag on every single mine, the game is won!!!</p>

<p>

Different types of cells:</p>

This cell has been clicked on, or has been triggered by a click. The number indicates how many bombs are next to the specific cell (in the case of the photo, only 1) in the 8 cells around the cell, forming a 3x3 grid.&nbsp;</p>

Revealed blank cell</p>
